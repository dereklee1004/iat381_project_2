I.D Tattoo Artisan is a multifaceted, art orientated business in downtown Vancouver, BC that promotes artists and their works by developing a diverse residency of alternative and multimedia artists and fostering interactions among those artists and commercial markets in a peer-based, community setting. We work with artists to position mixed media, new forms, cutting-edge and alternative art as a vibrant part of the Canadian cultural consciousness.
